Beijing, China: Qinghuayuan

A beautiful district nested in the northwestern suburbs of the city.

About Qinghuayuan

Beijing is famous for many attractions, though if you want a quieter day away from tourist crowds, you may consider stopping by the Haidian district surrounding renowned Qinghua University. The school itself hosts students from all over China and internationally, but the surrounding neighborhoods are lesser known. Majestic architecture, lush gardens, and a peaceful community can be found here.

Stroll in Qinghua Garden, with stone bridges and lotus ponds...

See the lotus blossoms in summertime at this park, which used to be part of historic Yuanmingyuan.

Bike around the peaceful neighborhood...

Enjoy peaceful and quiet streets made for biking, lined with lovely trees that change color with the seasons. You may even meet a stone lion!

See the Qinghua Auditorium

At the center of the west area of campus is the majestic Qinghua Auditorium, located at one end of the big lawn.
